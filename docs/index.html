About Us

CRAIC is a language learning platform that provide a place for learning and teaching foreign languages. With native speakers and two way learning methods that available on the website and mobile application, CRAIC aims to make learning and teaching foreign languages to be easy and fun.  By using “Find Your Language Buddy!” as a tagline, CRAIC also aims to broaden the e-network of students in Indonesia and all over the world. Providing free courses in their development year, CRAIC expected to be the most used educational platform which is easy, fun, and credible.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is CRAIC?

    CRAIC is a platform available on websites and mobile applications provided for college students in Indonesia to learn foreign languages with a native speaker. Using Two Way Learning method applied on chatting and video call.

  • What is the purpose of CRAIC?

    CRAIC aims to provide a new method of learning foreign language. So, learning can be more fun and easy by using mobile phone.

  • Who can use CRAIC and How The Role in it?

    The target users of CRAIC itself are students throughout Indonesia as well as foreign students. Where the role, each local and foreign student can be tutors and also participants. With the provision of teaching language of the student's home country.

  • What are the terms and conditions of being a participant/tutor of CRAIC?

    • Participants: all the participants should be a student from Indonesia proofed by identity card.
    • Tutors: all the tutors are a student/teacher for each foreign language should be a citizen of home country proofed by identity card.

  • How much fee for each courses in CRAIC?

    For the first year of CRAIC, all the courses held are free, and for the extra class besides the schedules/exceeded the specified times will be charge by the appointment of participants and tutor.
